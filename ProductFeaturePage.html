Ultrasonic Sensors

Four sensors placed around the device, helping the user to see the front, the back and the sides.

Image

A transmitter on each sensor sends waves. When the waves hit an object, some of them bounce back into the receiver.

Image

With today's technology we are only able to send the images to a chip inside a brain. Our goal is to implement the image into the brain without a chip.
